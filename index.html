ORIGIN STORY

HOW I GOT HERE

Trained as an aerospace engineer, radicalized by algorithms. I spent four years in Mumbai learning why things fall down (IIT Bombay, Aerospace Engineering) and two more in Michigan learning why things also sometimes bend, buckle, and break in interesting ways (University of Michigan, Mechanical Engineering).

Somewhere along the way I discovered that writing code that makes 3D geometry behave is more fun than writing equations that predict it — so now I work on production CAD software: hidden-line visibility, scenegraphs, exploded views, and a tool-using AI assistant that automates CAD drawing tasks. Outside of work I build small algorithm visualizers because apparently a day job full of geometry and code isn't enough geometry and code.

— still hasn't found aliens on Mars. yet.
